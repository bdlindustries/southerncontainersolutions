Outright Sales: Your Permanent Solution for Houma's Demanding Projects

When your projects demand a long-term, dependable on-site presence, our standard 20' and 40' container offices are the definitive choice. Built to endure, these units provide an immediate, high-quality workspace. Each office is meticulously outfitted with a suite of features designed for peak performance and occupant comfort:

  • Superior Climate Control: Say goodbye to the oppressive Houma heat. Our container offices feature 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, providing an energy-efficient barrier against the elements. Coupled with a powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, complete with surge protection for uninterrupted comfort even during power fluctuations, your team will stay cool, dry, and productive year-round.
  • Moisture & Durability: Louisiana's humidity is no match for our interiors. We utilize moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, ensuring a clean, mold-resistant environment that's easy to maintain.
  • Bright & Secure: Efficient LED lighting illuminates every corner, creating a productive workspace day or night. For peace of mind and protection of valuable equipment, each unit includes secure steel man doors, offering robust security against unauthorized access.
