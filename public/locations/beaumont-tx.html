Outright Sales: 20' and 40' Container Offices Ready for Deployment

Forget the limitations of traditional temporary structures that struggle under the intense Texas heat and humidity. Our standard 20' and 40' container offices are meticulously engineered to deliver unparalleled comfort and resilience, making them the ideal jobsite offices and essential mobile offices for any industrial, commercial, or construction project across Beaumont and the surrounding areas. We focus on delivering immediate solutions that empower your operations.

Each Southern Container Solutions unit is built for peak performance. They feature a generous 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, creating an incredibly energy-efficient envelope that drastically reduces cooling costs—a non-negotiable benefit during Beaumont's sweltering summers. Complementing this superior insulation is a powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump with surge protection, guaranteeing a consistently cool and comfortable environment, while also safeguarding against the power fluctuations common in demanding industrial settings. Your team will stay productive and protected from the elements year-round.

Inside, practicality meets durability. Our container offices boast easy-to-maintain moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, designed to defy the Gulf Coast's pervasive humidity and simplify cleaning. Bright, energy-efficient LED lighting ensures a well-lit workspace for precision tasks at any hour, and a robust, secure steel man door provides peace of mind, protecting your valuable equipment and personnel. Whether you are managing a refinery turnaround, overseeing a critical construction phase, or establishing a temporary command center for data center operations, these units are engineered for rapid deployment and lasting performance.
