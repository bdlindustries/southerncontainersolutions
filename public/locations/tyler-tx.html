Immediate & Durable Workspace for Tyler's Industries

Forget flimsy temporary structures. Our standard 20' and 40' container offices provide immediate, durable workspace solutions. These aren't just modified shipping containers; they're engineered environments. Each unit features a critical 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, ensuring your team stays cool through scorching Tyler summers and warm during unexpected cold snaps. This superior insulation drastically reduces energy costs, a vital consideration for any commercial operation.

Powering your comfort is a high-performance 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump with built-in surge protection. This robust system is specifically chosen to handle the unpredictable East Texas electrical environment, protecting your equipment and providing consistent climate control. Inside, you'll find easy-to-maintain, moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, designed to combat Tyler's notorious humidity and ensure a clean, professional aesthetic for years. Bright, energy-efficient LED lighting illuminates your workspace, reducing eye strain and operating costs. For security, every unit comes equipped with a dependable, secure steel man door, giving you peace of mind whether your container office is at a remote jobsite or an active industrial facility.
