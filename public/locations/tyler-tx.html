Unmatched Comfort and Durability for Your Tyler Operations

Each Southern Container Solutions office comes packed with features chosen specifically for performance and occupant comfort:

  • Superior Thermal Regulation: Enjoy consistent indoor temperatures thanks to 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation. This critical feature not only keeps the heat out (or in!) but also provides an excellent moisture barrier, a must-have in our humid East Texas environment.
  • Robust Climate Control: A powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump with integrated surge protection ensures efficient heating and cooling, safeguarding your equipment and operations from unexpected power fluctuations.
  • Low-Maintenance Interiors: Our interiors are clad with moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, making them incredibly easy to clean and highly durable against wear and tear, ideal for high-traffic industrial settings.
  • Bright, Efficient Lighting: Energy-efficient LED lighting illuminates your workspace, reducing energy costs and providing a clear, productive environment.
  • Enhanced Security: Each unit features a robust, secure steel man door, providing peace of mind for your valuable equipment and personnel after hours.
