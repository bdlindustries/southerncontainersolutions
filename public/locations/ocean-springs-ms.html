Engineered for Ocean Springs: Comfort and Durability

Southern Container Solutions understands the Ocean Springs climate: the intense summer heat, pervasive humidity, and sudden Gulf Coast storms. That's why every one of our container offices is meticulously equipped with features designed for superior comfort and longevity:

  • Optimal Climate Control: Enjoy year-round comfort thanks to 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation throughout the walls and ceiling. This superior insulation minimizes energy consumption and keeps the interior comfortable, even when the Ocean Springs sun beats down.
  • Powerful and Protected HVAC: Each office includes a high-efficiency 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, ensuring precise temperature regulation. Crucially for our storm-prone region, these units come with integrated surge protection to safeguard your investment against electrical fluctuations.
  • Moisture-Resistant Interiors: Say goodbye to mildew and maintenance headaches. Our interiors feature easy-to-clean, moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, making them ideal for humid conditions and ensuring a fresh, professional appearance for years.
  • Bright, Efficient Lighting: Experience clear, consistent illumination with energy-saving LED lighting installed throughout the office, reducing operating costs and enhancing productivity.
  • Superior Security: Protect your equipment and personnel with a heavy-duty, secure steel man door, providing robust access control for any industrial or commercial setting.

These features make our units the go-to solution for any Ocean Springs business seeking reliable jobsite offices or versatile mobile offices.
