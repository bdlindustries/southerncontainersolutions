Immediate Solutions: 20' and 40' Standard Container Offices for Sale

Why wait for conventional construction when your project demands immediate, reliable space? Southern Container Solutions provides a robust inventory of standard 20-foot and 40-foot container offices, available for outright purchase today. These aren't just modified shipping containers; they are expertly converted, climate-controlled environments designed for maximum productivity and comfort, even in the most challenging conditions.

  • Unrivaled Climate Control: Every unit features 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, creating a highly efficient envelope that drastically reduces energy costs and keeps interiors comfortable year-round. Paired with a powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, complete with essential surge protection, you're guaranteed a consistent, comfortable temperature, crucial for the Texas City heat and humidity.
  • Durable and Low-Maintenance Interiors: Our interiors are built to last. Moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels provide a clean, professional aesthetic that stands up to heavy use and the humid coastal air, requiring minimal upkeep.
  • Bright, Efficient Lighting: Modern LED lighting illuminates your workspace effectively, enhancing visibility and reducing energy consumption.
  • Security You Can Trust: Each container office is equipped with secure steel man doors, ensuring the safety of your equipment, documents, and personnel, a critical feature for any valuable jobsite office.

Whether you need a temporary command center, an on-site administrative hub, or a secure breakroom for your crew at the refinery or construction zone, our mobile offices are the perfect solution. They are delivered ready for utility hookup, providing instant functionality where and when you need it most.
