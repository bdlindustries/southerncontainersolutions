Each standard container office comes packed with features designed to combat the Columbus climate and enhance productivity, ensuring your team remains comfortable and efficient:

  • Superior Insulation: Enjoy consistent comfort and significant energy savings thanks to 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation applied throughout the walls and ceiling. This critical feature combats extreme temperatures, keeping your team cool in summer and warm in winter.
  • Reliable Climate Control: A powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump with surge protection provides optimal heating and cooling, designed to withstand power fluctuations common in industrial settings and keep your team productive year-round.
  • Durable Interior Finishes: Our interiors feature highly durable, moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels. These panels are incredibly easy to clean and maintain, resisting the humidity challenges often faced in Mississippi.
  • Bright & Efficient Lighting: Work under optimal conditions with energy-efficient LED lighting, ensuring a well-lit and comfortable workspace for blueprints, meetings, and daily tasks, even on the darkest days.
  • Enhanced Security: Protect your valuable equipment, documents, and personnel with a robust, secure steel man door. This provides crucial peace of mind on any jobsite, from remote industrial parks to busy urban developments.
