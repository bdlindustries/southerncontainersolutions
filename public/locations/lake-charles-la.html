Engineered for Lake Charles Climate and Industry

  • Unmatched Insulation: Each of our container offices features a full 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation. This critical detail provides superior thermal resistance, keeping interiors cool during sweltering summers and warm during unexpected cold snaps, significantly reducing energy costs for your operations near the Calcasieu Ship Channel or beyond.
  • Powerful Climate Control: Combat the Louisiana humidity and heat with 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pumps. These high-efficiency units, complete with surge protection, ensure a consistently comfortable interior, protecting your sensitive equipment and personnel from power fluctuations common in industrial settings.
  • Durable & Maintainable Interiors: Our interiors are clad with moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels. This choice is vital for the humid Lake Charles climate, preventing mold and mildew growth and ensuring a clean, professional, and low-maintenance environment for years of heavy use.
  • Bright & Efficient Lighting: Maximize productivity with energy-efficient LED lighting throughout, providing clear visibility and reducing utility expenses, essential for long shifts at refineries or data management centers.
  • Superior Security: Protecting your assets and personnel is paramount. Each unit includes secure steel man doors, offering robust security and peace of mind for valuable equipment and sensitive documents stored on site.
