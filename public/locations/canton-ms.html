Outright Sales: Your Permanent, High-Performance Workspace Solution

Ready to invest in an office solution that stands up to anything Canton throws at it? Southern Container Solutions provides standard 20' and 40' container offices available for outright purchase, delivered directly to your job site or commercial property. Each unit is meticulously crafted to ensure maximum comfort and operational efficiency, making them the perfect jobsite offices for supervisors, project managers, or administrative teams.

Our container offices are built with your comfort and productivity in mind, especially when facing Mississippi's humid summers and damp winters. Inside, you'll find a climate-controlled environment powered by a high-efficiency 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, complete with essential surge protection to safeguard against power fluctuations common in industrial settings. To maintain this ideal climate and energy efficiency, every office is insulated with 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, effectively sealing out the elements and reducing utility costs.

The interior walls and ceilings feature durable, moisture-resistant PVC panels, providing a clean, professional aesthetic that resists humidity and requires minimal maintenance – ideal for the local climate. Bright, energy-efficient LED lighting illuminates your workspace, ensuring visibility for all tasks. Security is paramount, which is why each unit is equipped with a robust, secure steel man door, offering peace of mind for your valuable equipment and sensitive documents. These highly adaptable units function perfectly as mobile offices, giving your operations flexibility.
