Outright Sales: Durable Container Offices Built for Louisiana

Forget the compromises of traditional temporary structures. Our standard 20' and 40' container offices are purpose-built for outright sale, offering unparalleled durability and long-term value. Each unit is a fortress against the elements, designed to withstand the intense heat, high humidity, and occasional severe weather common to the Gulf Coast. We understand the need for a comfortable retreat from the Laplace sun, which is why every office features an exceptional 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation. This critical feature not only provides superior thermal performance, keeping interiors cool and energy costs down, but also acts as an excellent moisture barrier, a must-have in our humid climate.

Comfort and productivity are paramount. That's why each of our container offices comes equipped with a high-efficiency 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, ensuring powerful cooling and heating when you need it most. Crucially, these units include built-in surge protection to safeguard your valuable electronics against power fluctuations common in industrial settings. Inside, you'll find bright, energy-efficient LED lighting and durable, low-maintenance moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, designed to stand up to the rigors of a busy jobsite. Security is also a top priority; each unit is fitted with robust, secure steel man doors, providing peace of mind for your equipment and personnel.
