  • Superior Climate Control: Each unit boasts 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, providing an exceptional thermal barrier against Laplace's sweltering summers and damp winters. This ensures lower energy costs and a consistently comfortable interior.
  • Reliable HVAC System: Stay cool and dry with a powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump. Crucially, every unit includes integrated surge protection, safeguarding your equipment and operations from power fluctuations common in industrial settings.
