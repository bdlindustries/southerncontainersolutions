Imagine a workspace that stands as an oasis against the Louisiana heat and humidity. Our container offices feature top-tier specifications to ensure unparalleled comfort and longevity:

  • Superior Insulation: Each unit boasts 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, creating an impenetrable thermal barrier that keeps the oppressive St. Francisville heat out and your conditioned air in.
  • Powerful Climate Control: Stay cool and comfortable with a high-efficiency 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump, complete with crucial surge protection to safeguard against power fluctuations common in industrial settings.
  • Moisture-Resistant Interiors: Our interiors are lined with durable, easy-to-clean moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, ensuring a pristine, mold-free environment that holds up against St. Francisville's persistent humidity.

  • Secure Access: Peace of mind comes standard with our robust, secure steel man doors, protecting your valuable equipment and confidential documents.
