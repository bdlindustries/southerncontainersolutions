Beat the Kenner Heat & Humidity with Our Superior Container Offices

We understand the critical need for a comfortable and productive environment, especially when the Kenner sun is beating down. Our standard container offices are engineered to combat the elements. Each unit features a robust 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, creating an airtight seal that dramatically reduces energy costs and keeps the interior cool and dry. Powering this comfort are high-efficiency 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pumps, complete with surge protection to safeguard against Kenner's occasional power fluctuations, ensuring your team stays productive no matter the weather outside.

Inside, you'll find an environment built for efficiency and longevity. Our container offices boast moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, perfect for Kenner's humid climate, preventing mold and mildew while being easy to clean. Bright, energy-efficient LED lighting illuminates your workspace, and for security and peace of mind, each unit is outfitted with secure steel man doors, protecting your equipment and personnel.
