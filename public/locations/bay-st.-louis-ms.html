Outright Sales: Your Permanent Solution for Jobsite & Mobile Offices

Invest in quality that lasts. Southern Container Solutions offers direct sales of our meticulously crafted container offices, ready to deploy. Each unit is built to provide an immediate, high-performance workspace, perfectly suited as jobsite offices or versatile mobile offices across Bay St. Louis. We understand the specific requirements of our local industries, and our containers are equipped accordingly:

  • Superior Climate Control: Featuring 2 inches of closed-cell spray foam insulation, our units are exceptionally well-sealed against the intense Gulf Coast heat and humidity, ensuring a stable interior temperature.
  • Efficient HVAC & Protection: Stay cool and comfortable with a powerful 12,000 BTU LG mini-split heat pump with surge protection, designed to handle the variable power demands of industrial settings and protect against electrical fluctuations.
  • Durable & Maintainable Interiors: The interior boasts moisture-resistant PVC wall and ceiling panels, ensuring longevity, easy cleaning, and resistance to the humid Bay St. Louis air.
  • Bright & Energy-Efficient Lighting: Equipped with modern LED lighting, our offices provide excellent illumination while keeping energy consumption low, a crucial factor for long-term operations.
  • Enhanced Security: Your equipment and personnel are protected by a robust, secure steel man door, offering peace of mind on any jobsite.
