Recent Delivery: Custom Container Office supporting Venture Global Plaquemines LNG Project

Documented delivery of a specialized 40ft container office to the active Plaquemines LNG construction site, providing secure climate controlled workspace for specialized workforce operations in Port Sulphur.

Southern Container Solutions built and delivered a custom specialized container office to support operations at the Venture Global Plaquemines LNG project in Port Sulphur, Louisiana. This secure, climate controlled unit provides reliable infrastructure on one of the largest active industrial construction sites in Plaquemines Parish, giving field teams a hardened workspace that matches the scale and intensity of LNG export development on the Gulf Coast.

Remote Site Office Port Sulphur Built for LNG Scale Operations

Remote site office Port Sulphur crews need more than a temporary trailer when schedules run continuously and specialty trades rotate through the gate daily. This 40ft high cube container office was configured at our yard with a steel personnel door, barred windows, exterior entry lighting, and a dedicated HVAC package before transport to the Plaquemines LNG site. The tan industrial finish and one trip container shell hold up to heavy yard traffic, Gulf humidity, and the nonstop pace of a multi billion dollar energy project.

Ground level placement on a prepared gravel pad gives immediate walk in access without stairs or trailer ramps. Project coordinators, safety leads, and specialty contractors use the interior as a secure base for plans, radios, and laptops while material handling continues across the surrounding site.

Custom Features on This Specialized Build

The side entry man door sits between two sliding windows protected by welded steel security bars. An exterior photocell light above the door supports early shift call and late night closeout. The end wall carries the mini split condenser and electrical service in weatherproof enclosures, keeping climate control and power distribution organized on the exterior where site electricians can tie in quickly.

Plaquemines Parish LNG Logistics Support on Active Construction

Plaquemines Parish LNG logistics support demands infrastructure that arrives ready to work. We loaded this unit at our Covington area yard, ran it downriver through the Plaquemines corridor, and set it on site at Port Sulphur. Tilt bed delivery dropped the office flat on crushed stone without blocking crane lanes or staging pads that stay active around the clock.

Container offices integrate cleanly into large LNG construction footprints. They do not require blocking packages, skirting, or elevated landings that eat turnaround space. When workforce counts surge during peak construction phases, additional units can stage in the same pattern to expand office capacity without permanent foundations.

Primary Port Sulphur delivery routes: Louisiana Highway 23, Louisiana Highway 39, and Plaquemines Parish industrial access roads serving the PLNG construction corridor.

Secure Workforce Solutions for Demanding Industrial Environments

Secure workforce solutions on LNG megaprojects mean lockable steel enclosures, controlled interior climate, and reliable power for the devices crews depend on. This build ships with 2 inch closed cell spray foam insulation, moisture resistant interior panels, and a 12,000 BTU LG mini split heat pump sized for Louisiana heat loads. The 100 amp breaker panel feeds interior LED lighting and multiple outlets for networking gear, printers, and charging stations.

Welded window bars and a deadbolted personnel door protect documents, instruments, and personal equipment between shifts. Operational rear cargo doors allow larger items to move through when needed. The result is a self contained office module that supports specialized trades without tying up permanent plant buildings still under construction.

Reliable Infrastructure Where Permanent Buildings Are Not Ready

On active PLNG construction, permanent structures lag behind field operations. Container offices bridge that gap with factory controlled build quality, predictable delivery timelines, and the flexibility to relocate as the project footprint evolves. Southern Container Solutions delivers that infrastructure so workforce teams focus on the work in front of them instead of fighting inadequate temporary space.
