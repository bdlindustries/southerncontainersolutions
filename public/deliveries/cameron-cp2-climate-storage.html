Recent Delivery: Custom Climate-Controlled Storage Container at Venture Global CP2 LNG Site

Documented ground level delivery of a 40ft climate-controlled storage unit to support logistics and protect sensitive equipment on the active CP2 construction project in Cameron Parish.

Southern Container Solutions recently delivered a custom climate-controlled storage container directly to the Venture Global CP2 LNG construction site in Cameron, Louisiana. This unit was built to support active job site logistics and protect temperature-sensitive materials, instruments, and spare equipment on one of the largest industrial projects on the Louisiana Gulf Coast.

Remote Site Storage Cameron: Built for Active LNG Construction

Large scale LNG export projects like CP2 run around the clock with thousands of workers, heavy equipment, and tight material schedules. Remote site storage Cameron contractors can trust has to do more than keep rain off pallets. This climate-controlled unit provides a secure, enclosed environment where sensitive components stay organized and protected while the surrounding pad stays open for cranes, forklifts, and haul roads.

The container sits flat on a compacted gravel and shell laydown pad typical of Cameron Parish industrial yards. Ground level placement means no trailer stairs, no skirting, and no wasted turnaround space. Crews walk straight in through the personnel door while heavy lift operations continue across the site.

Equipment Protection CP2 Demands on the Gulf Coast

CP2 is an active construction environment with steel staging, crane operations, and constant vehicle traffic. Storing electronics, calibrated instruments, specialty coatings, and spare parts in an unconditioned steel box is not an option on the Louisiana coast. This build was engineered specifically for equipment protection CP2 schedules require: controlled temperature, managed humidity, and a locked enclosure that stays stable shift after shift.

HVAC Container Solutions for Louisiana Heat and Humidity

Cameron Parish sits in one of the most demanding climates in the country for stored equipment. Summer heat, Gulf humidity, and salt air push unprotected enclosures into condensation and corrosion fast. HVAC container solutions have to hold setpoint through open-door loading, generator power fluctuations, and long idle weekends on site.

This unit ships with a high-output industrial HVAC system mounted on a reinforced exterior bracket with dedicated electrical service. Closed-cell spray foam insulation wraps the entire envelope so the system is not fighting radiant heat through bare corrugated steel. The result is a stable interior environment that protects inventory worth far more than the container itself.

Combating Coastal Humidity Inside the Unit

Every surface inside was treated for moisture control. Spray foam fills the corrugations and frames the cargo doors. Interior lining panels finish the walls for a clean, wipe-down surface that resists tracking mud and grit from the yard. Combined with LED lighting and a full outlet package, the space functions as a true conditioned storage room rather than a modified shipping box.

Delivery Logistics to the CP2 Site in Cameron Parish

We dispatched this container on heavy transport and placed it directly on the prepared laydown surface at the CP2 jobsite. Tilt bed and lowboy options both work depending on site access and pad conditions. Our drivers coordinate with site security and traffic control common on active LNG construction projects so the drop happens safely without blocking crane lanes or material staging.

Primary Cameron delivery routes: Louisiana Highway 27 and Louisiana Highway 82.

Site electricians tie temporary power from a jobsite generator or site distribution panel into the exterior breaker package. From there, the HVAC condenser, interior outlets, and lighting run off a single coordinated electrical system designed for field use.

Build Specifications on This CP2 Delivery

  • 40ft custom shipping container built from a one-trip high-cube unit in excellent structural condition
  • High-output industrial HVAC system for full climate and humidity control
  • Closed-cell spray foam insulation package (R-value optimized for Gulf Coast heat loads)
  • Complete electrical package with breaker panel, interior outlets, and LED lighting
  • Secure lockbox and security personnel door for controlled site access
  • Interior lining with finished wall panels over insulated substrate
  • Exterior-mounted condenser on reinforced steel bracket with weatherproof conduit
  • Operational rear cargo doors for loading oversized equipment and palletized materials
  • Ground level placement with no ramps, stairs, or trailer blocking required
  • Dark charcoal exterior finish suited for industrial construction environments
